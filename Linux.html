Why Linux?


Linux is everywhare: in smart phones, cars, supercomputers and home appliances. This OS has been around since the mid '90s and has reached a user-base that spans industries and continents. It runs most of the internet and even the worlds stock exchange. It is the most reliable, secure, and worry-free operating system available!

Still not convinved?

Does the operating system on your computer work 'just fine'? Or does it slow down often, get viruses, and crashes? If you are afraid of losing data, Linux is for you! . Linux has evolved into one of the most reliable computer ecosystems on the planet . Also this operating system is free and easy to install.

What exactly does Linux help with?

It allows for your computer to boot, manages the CPU, memory, and periphelal devices. Also, it does background services like printing, sound, schedualling and so on. It allows you to see vedios and applications among other things.
